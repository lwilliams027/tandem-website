How It Works
Dialing in 5 steps

No training required. No IT department. Install and start dialing in under 5 minutes.

1

Install the Extension

Add Tandem from the Chrome Web Store. One click, takes 10 seconds.

2

Log In

Open the extension popup, enter your credentials. Your phone number and settings are configured automatically.

3

Navigate to Your Leads

Go to Impact Portal like normal. Tandem's floating panel appears on the right side of the page.

4

Click Start

Tandem auto-dials the lead, detects voicemails, and advances through your list. When someone answers, you hear a chime.

5

Talk, Log, Repeat

Have your conversation. Log the result in Impact. Click Resume — Tandem dials the next lead.

FAQ
Common questions
What do I need to use Tandem?+

Google Chrome, an Impact Portal login, and a microphone (your laptop's built-in mic works fine). That's it — no desk phone, no extra software, no hardware.

How long does setup take?+

Under 5 minutes. Install the Chrome extension, log in, and you're dialing. Your phone number is provisioned automatically when you sign up.

Does it work with my existing Impact workflow?+

Yes — Tandem runs as a floating panel on top of Impact. It reads your leads, makes calls, and logs dispositions using Impact's own interface. Nothing changes about how you use Impact.

How does voicemail detection work?+

Tandem uses AI-powered answering machine detection (AMD). When a voicemail greeting is detected, the call is automatically ended and logged — no wasted time listening to greetings.

Can I keep my area code?+

Yes. When you sign up, choose your preferred area code and we'll provision a local number for you. Need to change it later? It's $15 for a new number with any area code.

Can my team leader see my stats?+

Yes. Team leaders get a dashboard showing every agent's daily dials, answers, voicemails, and contact rate. Great for coaching and accountability.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel from the extension and your plan stays active until the end of your billing period.
