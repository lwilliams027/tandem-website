FAQ

Got questions?

What do I need to use Tandem?
+
Just Google Chrome and an active Impact Portal login. No downloads, no softphones, no special hardware. Tandem is a Chrome extension that works on any computer — Mac, PC, or Chromebook.
How does voicemail detection work?
+
Tandem uses four parallel detection signals: carrier-level AMD, audio beep detection, transcript phrase matching, and behavioral timers. When a voicemail is detected, the call is automatically skipped so you only spend time talking to live people.
Will my leads know I'm using an auto-dialer?
+
No. When a live person picks up, Tandem unmutes your mic seamlessly. From the prospect's perspective, it sounds like a normal phone call. There's no awkward delay or robotic click-through.
What is the AI rebuttal coach?
+
Tandem listens to the call in real time using speech-to-text. When the prospect raises an objection — like "I'm not interested" or "I already have coverage" — the AI instantly suggests a tailored rebuttal on your screen. Think of it as a cheat sheet that updates live.
Can I choose my area code?
+
Yes! You're assigned a number during setup, and you can request a number change for $15. Local presence — where your caller ID matches the prospect's area code — is coming soon at $50 per state.
How do team plans work?
+
Team plans give every agent unlimited dials at a discounted rate. Managers get access to the team dashboard with live stats across all agents. Plans are available for 5, 10, 15, or 20 agents.
Can I cancel anytime?
+
Yes. All plans are month-to-month with no contracts. Cancel anytime from your account settings and your subscription stops at the end of the billing period.
