FAQ

Got questions?

What do I need to use Tandem?
+
Just Google Chrome and an active Impact Portal login. No downloads, no softphones, no special hardware. Tandem works on any computer — Mac, PC, or Chromebook.
How does voicemail detection work?
+
Tandem uses four parallel detection signals: carrier-level AMD, audio beep detection, transcript phrase matching, and behavioral timers. Voicemails are skipped automatically.
Will my leads know I'm using an auto-dialer?
+
No. When a live person picks up, Tandem unmutes your mic seamlessly. It sounds like a normal phone call — no delay, no robotic click-through.
What is the AI rebuttal coach?
+
Tandem listens in real time using speech-to-text. When the prospect raises an objection, the AI instantly suggests a tailored rebuttal on your screen. Available on the Gold plan.
Can I choose my area code?
+
Yes! You're assigned a number during setup. Number changes are $15.
How do team plans work?
+
Team plans give every agent unlimited dials at a discounted rate. Managers get the team dashboard. Plans for 5, 10, 15, or 20 agents.
Can I cancel anytime?
+
Yes. Month-to-month, no contracts. Cancel anytime and your subscription stops at the end of the billing period.
